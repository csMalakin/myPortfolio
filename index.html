What you can expect working with me

Consultation

Our partnership begins with a "coffee chat", where we discuss every detail of the project you are envisioning - no project idea is off-limits.

Proposal

After we discuss your project, I will follow up with a proposal. This is where I compile all of your ideas. My job is to ensure I can deliver a finished product within a reasonable timeframe and within your desired budget, all while meeting your needs.

Creation

From this point, I will begin developing your website or app. I will contact you with regular updates on what I'm working on. During this process, you will have ample time to interact and experience the product to ensure your satisfaction.

Launch & Support

Upon completion, we will launch your website!. Client relationships become long-term partnerships in an ongoing process to improve your site and provide support.

About Me

I was given the chance to do a training to become a certified "Fachinformatiker für Anwendungsentwicklung" (freely translated it means something like "IT specialist for application developemnet") which I will successfully complete at the end of this year.
